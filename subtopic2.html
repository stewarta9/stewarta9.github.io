Abstract The combination of CAD, CAM, and CIM is what makes manufacturing clothes so much more efficient. It also makes it easier to create consistent quality products. The combination of these three systems also make it easier for companies to adjust to the swift change in trends. Computer Aided Manufacturing and Computer Aided Design allows designers to make changes to designs or patterns without having to start from scratch. CAD also allows designers to use softwares like 3D visualizations to make sure that the designs are detailed and as desired. CAM creates the designs into data and actually manufactures the designs.Computer Intergrated Manufacturing reads the CAD data and make sure that each machinery completes it's desired tasks efficiently and with the least amount of time. Computer Aided Design, Manufacturing, and Intergrated Manufacturing have become the new standard in order to compete in the ever growing fashion industry.
